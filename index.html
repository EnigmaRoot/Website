About Me

Hello! I'm Dipesh Dhakal, a passionate cybersecurity specialist based in Kathmandu, Nepal. Currently pursuing my BSc. (Hons) in Ethical Hacking and Cyber Security at Coventry University.

When I'm not analyzing security protocols or participating in CTF challenges, I enjoy engaging in cybersecurity forums and sharing my knowledge with peers through security awareness training sessions.
